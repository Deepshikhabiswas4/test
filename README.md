The dark backward and abyss of time has led me through an open mind,
where i take control in bits and bits, 
of the old life that still exists.

time, passing time has led me to this part 
i clear the mess, the dead, and rest 
Of the old dust, of quantity and any sign of grace.
the wind still lingers on the tip of my fingers, just a bit more before it becomes "too long" 
i have seen this film before, in my dream or is it deja vu?

i unfold as a seedling, taking shelter
under a tremendous banyan tree.
i learn and unlearn all that ever caused me pain and distress. 
i let go of it all. 
i see what was, and what is. what will be is still unknown
 
the unknown has always made me weak. so all i ever do now is let go.
letting go has been my biggest lesson this lifetime.
i soak in the sun rays falling on my face, i close my eyes now, i let go of any breaths left. 

i see the world for what it actually is
i see the world squishing into a small portion of something still unknown to me. in a second, im somewhere else now. this place is quite big, empty, there are big trees, i hear the Psithurism as i fly around them.
the abyss of time has led me to this new life. i dont quite understand yet. am i dead? am i in the lifetime? the look of life has definitely changed.
